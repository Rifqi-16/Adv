About Red Bull Racing Team

Red Bull Racing is a Formula 1 team owned by the Austrian company Red Bull GmbH. Since its debut in 2005, the team has evolved into one of the most successful and innovative teams in the sport. Based in Milton Keynes, UK, Red Bull Racing has become known for its aggressive technical developments, strong driver lineup, and iconic branding.
